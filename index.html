Privacy Policy for Media Saver Pro

Effective date: 2026-05-08 · Last updated: 2026-05-08

This Privacy Policy describes how the Media Saver Pro mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. By installing and using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Summary

Media Saver Pro is designed to work entirely on your device.

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not have user accounts, sign-in, or cloud sync.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising networks.
  • The media you save and view stays on your device. It is never uploaded to our servers (we do not operate any servers).

2. Information we access on your device

To provide the App's core features, we access certain device resources locally only:

ResourcePurposeSent off-device?
Photos & Videos (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO)Display files you have saved into the in-app Gallery and register newly downloaded media with your system galleryNo
Storage Access Framework folder grantRead media files from a folder you explicitly select (used by the Status feature)No
InternetFetch trending photos/videos from Pexels and Pixabay; download media when you paste a public URL into the in-app browserSee section 3

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, calendar, SMS, call logs, or any other sensitive data.

3. Third-party services

Media Saver Pro makes network requests to the following services to power the Trending and Browser features:

  • Pexels (pexels.com) — stock photo and video search. See their privacy policy.
  • Pixabay (pixabay.com) — stock photo and video search. See their privacy policy.
  • Public media URLs that you choose to paste into the in-app browser.

When the App calls these services, the network request includes only the search query you typed and standard HTTP headers (such as your IP address and User-Agent, which any HTTP request reveals). We do not attach a user identifier, device identifier, or account information.

4. Information we do not collect

We do not collect, log, or transmit:

  • Your name, email, phone number, or any account information
  • Your device advertising ID
  • Your precise or approximate location
  • Crash reports or analytics events
  • The content, filenames, or metadata of files you save or view

5. Permissions we explicitly do not request

The following permissions are blocked in the App's manifest:

  • Camera (CAMERA)
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)
  • Audio media access (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO)
  • Legacy external storage (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)

6. Data retention

Because we do not collect or store any data about you on our servers, there is no retention policy on our side. Files saved by the App live on your device until you delete them (either inside the App or via the system gallery).

7. Children's privacy

Media Saver Pro is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used the App and you have a concern, please contact us at the email below and we will respond promptly.

8. Your rights

Because we do not store your data, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. To remove all App data from your device:

  1. Open Settings → Apps → Media Saver Pro → Storage → Clear data, or
  2. Uninstall the App.

9. Security

All network requests made by the App use HTTPS. Files saved by the App are stored in the App's private storage and the system gallery, governed by Android's standard sandboxing.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
